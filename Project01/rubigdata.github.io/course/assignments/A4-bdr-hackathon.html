RU Big Data 2019 (NWI-IBC036)

Assignment 4 BDR Hackathon

Building a recommender with BigData Republic

Taking part in the Hackaton is mandatory, evaluated as pass/fail - so your grade does not depend on winning this challenge. Who would not want to celebrate passing the course with the eternal fame of having achieved the best recommendation results (class of 2020)?!

Proceed as follows:

  1. Decide whether to work as a couple, or individually;
  2. Accept the invitation from our github classroom (just once);
  3. you find all you need in the assignment repo; copy the notebook into the rubigdata/hadoop docker container and get going;
  4. have fun!

For this assignment, it is allowed to work in pairs (but not obligatory) - coordinate on choosing a team-name prior to accepting the invite to the assignment, and you can share a repository (max two students per repository).
